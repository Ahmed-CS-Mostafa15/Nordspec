OIL & PETROCHEMICALS

Nordspec responds to the needs of major oil and petrochemical industry players through an expansive global network of testing facilities.

YOUR CHALLENGES

Quality control services are crucial to the oil and petrochemicals industry, and to those who use their products. Oil, gas and petrochemical producers and traders need to be certain of the quality of their product, and ensure its value is preserved. And airlines, ship owners and vendors of auto fuel need to be sure fuel and lubricants are on-specification.

HOW WE SUPPORT YOU

We offer inspection, testing and consultancy services at loading, discharge and ship-to-ship locations around the world. Our clients benefit from our global network of state-of-the-art laboratory testing facilities.


1. Inspection Consultancy

Inspection Consultancy provides technical guidance for planning, reviewing, and improving inspection programs. The service is focused on supporting compliance, reducing inspection-related risk, and improving the reliability of oil and gas assets through structured inspection management.

2. NDT / ANDT

NDT / ANDT services use conventional and advanced non-destructive testing techniques to identify defects and assess material condition without damaging the asset. These services support inspection activities across equipment, piping, and welded components.

3. API Inspections

API Inspection services are performed in accordance with applicable API codes and standards to support equipment safety, compliance, and operational reliability. These services cover key asset categories commonly used in oil and gas facilities.

4. Asset Integrity

Asset Integrity services help evaluate equipment condition and support the long-term management of asset reliability and safety. The service covers assessment, planning, monitoring, and reporting activities needed to manage integrity throughout the equipment lifecycle.

5. Fitness-for-Service Level 1, 2, and 3

Fitness-for-Service (FFS) assessments determine whether equipment is suitable for continued operation based on its current condition. The service includes different levels of assessment depending on the complexity of the evaluation required.

6. Risk-Based Inspection

Risk-Based Inspection (RBI) services help prioritize inspection activities based on risk. The service is intended to support better inspection planning, optimize resource allocation, and improve integrity management through risk evaluation and data analysis.

7. Welding Inspection

Welding Inspection services verify weld quality, dimensional accuracy, and compliance with applicable requirements during fabrication and installation. The service also includes inspection and documentation activities to support weld acceptance and quality control.

8. Tube Inspection

Tube Inspection services are used to detect degradation, assess tube condition, and support the reliability of heat exchangers, boilers, and condensers. The service includes multiple testing methods and condition reporting for tube systems.

FACILITATING TRADE TRANSACTIONS

Professional inspection services are critical to custody of goods transfers between buyers and sellers of petroleum cargo. Nordspec offers certificates that are trusted for their integrity by all contractual parties, including financial institutions and insurance companies.
